It's an 8-inch Night Watch Newtonian Reflector Telescope with a Dobsonian mount from Guan Sheng Optical (GSO), Taiwan, and supplied by Tejraj & Co., Mumbai, India. We also got a solar filter, a Canon T ring M42 and a GSO 2x SA Barlow 1.25" to do astrophotography using a Canon DSLR with the telescope, and a universal mobile adapter to do astrophotography using smartphones with the telescope. This kit of equipment is extraordinary for beginners to learn optical astronomy observation techniques as well as optical astrophotography.
