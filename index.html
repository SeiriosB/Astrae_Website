Astrae is IISc's undergraduate astronomy and astrophysics club, a vibrant student community sharing a collective passion for the cosmos.

Events

From night sky explorations that invite everyone to marvel at the cosmos, to engaging talks on topics like stellar structure and evolution, celestial mechanics, and the dynamics of galaxies, Astrae brings complex ideas within reach of all. Whether you’re deeply curious about astrophysics or simply eager to experience the beauty of the night sky, Astrae is the right place for you. Alongside lectures, workshops, and hands-on activities, Astrae fosters collaboration, creativity, and community. At its heart, the club exists to bring the IISc community together across disciplines, through a shared passion for learning, discovery, and ultimately, a desire to understand the heavens above.
